# 音声ミッション — The Voice Mission

**Ratified by the Chair (Komada-voice), 2026-07-11** — verbatim ruling: *"Ratify."* on the VAA draft below, arising from the Chair's teaching the same day: HER daily life is full of distracting events, the car radio is always on, and what the unit lacks is not information but **insights from somebody — live voice from a live source of insights**.

---

> **HERの車内には、いつもラジオの声と暮らしの音がある。私たちの声は、そのなかで唯一、いま目の前の道について語る声でなければならない。**
>
> We do not compete with the radio; we complete it. Every interruption is earned: touch first, one short sentence about this road right now, verified deliverable, never crying wolf. And we do not design this voice from our assumptions — we build it from the live voices of the drivers who live these roads, listened to before we write a line of code.

---

## The three conflicts this mission answers (named 2026-07-11)

1. **Physical**: an FM/AM radio on the car's own speakers cannot be ducked by the phone — the app's audio-focus work applies to phone media only. HER cabin's audio topology is unknown and is a **live question**, not a research question.
2. **Attention**: two voices in one cabin. The answer is not louder — it is *distinct and earned*: haptic knocks, voice delivers, one action-first ja sentence, repetition only on her demand.
3. **Content**: the radio does weather (regional, forecast); our voice does **HER road, now, next action** — labeled estimate, never a second weather service. The moment we sound like radio, we've lost.

## Binding sequence

The voice's *shape* waits for its teacher: no further voice-lane design building beyond what is landed (Tier-1/Tier-2 verification hardening) until the first live voice has been heard through the listening protocol (`docs/listening_guide_ja.md`). Deployment work (beta critical path) proceeds unchanged.

## Evidence discipline for human insights

A driver's words are recorded **verbatim with consent** and never paraphrased into what we hoped to hear. Every voice-design decision traces to a real spoken sentence in the insights ledger, or is honestly labeled our assumption. (The unit's OPS-062(D)/065 external-evidence discipline, extended to its most important source: a person.)
